Brief history

Established by the Africa Inland Mission (AIM) in the early 20th century, Githumu Boys High School began as a pivotal mission station in the heart of Kandara, Murang’a County, serving as a center for both spiritual and academic enlightenment. Over the decades, the institution evolved from an intermediate missionary school into a premier secondary boarding school, gaining a reputation for molding disciplined and visionary young men. Set against the serene, rolling hills of Central Kenya, the school has transitioned into a modern academic powerhouse that balances a rigorous KCSE curriculum with vibrant success in the creative arts and drama. Today, Githumu Boys stands as a testament to its rich colonial-era roots and its enduring commitment to excellence, having produced generations of leaders who continue to impact the socio-economic landscape of Kenya.

Mission

To provide a safe environment for our learners to realize their potential and become useful in the society.

Principal
Remarks

Welcome to the official website of Githumu Boys High School.

As a premier Extra-County institution with a deep-rooted history in missionary excellence, our mission transcends mere academic instruction; we are dedicated to the holistic 'molding of men.' Our school stands as a beacon of discipline and hard work in the heart of Murang’a County, providing a serene environment where students are challenged to reach their full potential.

Furthermore, Githumu Boys stands at the forefront of educational transformation as a specialized hub for Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) implementation. We take pride in our role as a training center for educators across the region, fostering a collaborative environment where teachers are equipped with the pedagogical tools necessary for the 21st century. Through state-of-the-art resources and proactive faculty development, we are fully prepared and committed to realizing the success of the CBC, ensuring our students transition seamlessly into a world that values practical skills, critical thinking, and innovation.

In today’s rapidly changing world, we pride ourselves on a curriculum that balances rigorous KCSE preparation with vibrant co-curricular programs in drama, sports, and the arts. We believe that every boy who passes through our gates carries the legacy of our founders—built on a foundation of integrity, resilience, and a commitment to service.

Excellence is our tradition, and character is our strength."
